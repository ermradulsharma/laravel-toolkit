## Lead Maintainers

- **Mradul Sharma** ([@ermradulsharma](https://github.com/ermradulsharma)) - Lead Developer
- **Skywalker Labs Team** ([@skywalker-labs](https://github.com/skywalker-labs))

## Contributing Maintainers

We are always looking for community maintainers. If you are interested in helping maintain this critical foundation, please reach out via a GitHub issue or follow the guidelines in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Responsibility

Maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests.
- Ensuring the package adheres to PHP 7.0+ compatibility rules.
- Maintaining the PHPStan Level 9 baseline.
- Triaging issues and providing support.
